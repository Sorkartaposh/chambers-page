what we offer

After managing more than 700 incidents, scheduled and unscheduled projects, from oil releases to train derailments, we know how to help clients prepare, plan, respond and recover quickly.

  • • Emergency and Crisis Management
  • • Environmental Regulatory Analysis and Compliance
  • • Planning and Preparedness, Exercise, Training
  • • Staffing needs for Unscheduled Events/ Projects.
